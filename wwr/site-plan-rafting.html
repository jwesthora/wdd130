Overview

Purpose

[The purpose for this website is to bring people from all walks of life together in a unified love for the outdoors. Developing a source for all outdoor lovers to engage in sharing memories, tips, and pictures of their adventures in order to help others plan their next adventure. While utilizing tools from this website, you will improve your ability to grow and learn from one another in how to find those hidden gems that will result in a lifetime memory of thrilling experiances.t.]

Audience

[Anyone who is looking to enjoy the outdoors individually or with others in hopes of increasing connections. From young and wise to begginner to expert, anyone seeking adventure and trying new things in the glory of nature. Those needing maps, tips, tricks on nature canoeing, rafting, hiking, biking, mountain climbing, rock climbing and so much more. We have everything you will need to plan your trip, from maps, direction, pictures, video and tips and tricks in your adventure meeting or exeeding your expectation. We are easily accessed through any device that is capable of the internet and have a downloadable app to carry with you on your phone.]
